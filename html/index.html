About Me

Upon leaving the Military and studying Mathematics and Computer Science for 2 years at St Petersburg College, I entered the IT Industry as a System Adminstrator. On the job, I was forced to learn Java, HTML, and CSS very quickly to make my job, and everyone's in the IT department, simpler and less stressful. After a year of working with these languages daily, and programming in my spare time, I decided to switch career paths and to fill the gaps that self-taught learning may have left.

To further my knowledge of Java(and other C based languages), HTML5, CSS3 and to learn Bootstrap, JavaScript, AngularJS(all of which I used to make this website), and a litany of other languages, I decided to attend the Skill Distillery in Denver, CO.

I am now a certified Java Developer through Oracle(OCP). I also have a working knowledge of C++, Python and C#. You can see the progression of my programming career via the Projects section I have below and the Github page in the Contacts section.

I appreciate you visiting my website, and look forward to hearing from you if you are in need of a Front-End, Back-End, or Full Stack Developer.
